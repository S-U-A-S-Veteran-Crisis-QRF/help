# Help Wanted — Volunteer with SUAS Veteran Crisis QRF

> S.U.A.S. Veteran Crisis QRF is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit working to combat veteran isolation through peer support and community events. We are looking for skilled volunteers to help us reach more veterans.

## Why this work matters

Veteran suicide kills roughly 17 veterans every day in the United States. Isolation is one of the largest drivers. SUAS exists to bridge that gap — BBQs, surfing, hiking, hunting, and simple stand-down events that pull veterans back into community.

We are a small team. Every hour a volunteer puts in is an hour we get to spend on the mission instead of on tech logistics.

## How to get involved

### One: pick something
Browse [open issues labeled `good first issue` or `help wanted`](https://github.com/S-U-A-S-Veteran-Crisis-QRF/help/issues?q=is%3Aopen+label%3A%22good+first+issue%22%2C%22help+wanted%22). Comment on the issue you want to claim.

### Two: read the contributor guide
[`CONTRIBUTING.md`](./CONTRIBUTING.md) covers what to do (and what to be careful about, since we serve veterans in crisis).

### Three: ship the change
Open a pull request. We will review and merge.

## What we will not ask of volunteers

- We will not ask you for money.
- We will not ask you for access to anyone's personal computer.
- We will not ask you to interact with veterans in crisis without explicit training and consent — that work goes through trained peer counselors.
