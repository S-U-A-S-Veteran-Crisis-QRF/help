Pilot overview

The pilot tests whether structured check-ins and trusted-circle coordination help surface support needs earlier — not clinical diagnosis, just peer check-in.

  • Duration: 12 weeks (planned Q4 2026)
  • Cohort size: 25–50 veterans
  • Privacy: All data encrypted. No PII shared outside consent.
  • Commitment: Two peer check-ins per month + optional group call

Pilot readiness
