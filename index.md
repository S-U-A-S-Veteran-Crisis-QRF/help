# S.U.A.S. Veteran Crisis QRF

**Shut Up And Serve** — Veterans Crisis Quick Reaction Force

*Getting between veterans, military isolation, and the war within.*

---

## Our Mission

S.U.A.S. Veteran Crisis QRF is a registered charitable nonprofit dedicated to serving veterans, active-duty military, and their families. We combat isolation — the silent threat behind the veteran suicide crisis — by bringing warriors together in environments where they can stand down, reconnect, and find their people again.

## What We Do

### Peer Counseling & Resource Navigation

We connect veterans with peer counselors who have been there, and bridge them to the nonprofits and resources that fit their situation.

### Stand-Down Events

Free cookouts on military bases and installations — no agenda, no pressure, just brothers and sisters in arms breaking bread together.

### Get Outside, Get Connected

We pull veterans out of isolation through shared experience:

- BBQs and cookouts
- Camping trips
- Surfing
- Hunting and fishing
- Hiking
- Skydiving

The activities are the excuse. The connection is the point.
